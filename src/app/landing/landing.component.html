Introduction

This program was developed for Siemens Offshore in hopes of reaching a milestone in regards to an internal problem with monitoring.
The purpose of the program is to allow maintaining a close look over where rigs / platforms are at any given moment. Apart from this crucial information, the program also informs users of relevant information such as the type of the rig as well as the area it's located in (if provided by the designated AIS) and the status of the chosen rig/platform
